Thoughts on Apple Vision Pro

8/10 - First Gen Product. But,



If the Vision Pro was made of plastic instead of glass and aluminum and weighs half it is now, will the experience be better ???
Is flashy-ness holding us back from a good product and a great experience ?
If it was half the weight, they could have put in the battery inside and made the pocket battery optional !
Just imagine introducing Spacitial Computing to the world without a hanging cable !
Vision Pro : light , powerful , $2499 (because of no glass and aluminum).
Remove the entire lenticular display replace it with bright oled showing memoji eyes with movement.
Vision Pro : light , powerful , fun , $1999 !

Anyway,
Apple Vision Pro One line review: It’s the best Mixed Reality headset but that’s not match for the real world “reality” much like its price of $3500.
It’s the best that’s ever been. No one’s even close.
It’s a 7/10 review from Verge.
That might look only above average. But think about it.
7/10 for “Replacing Reality” !
AND augmenting it with useful software features !!!
Doing 7/10 job of THAT is a “pretty good start” !
Both Meta and Apple headsets are good in their own ways
But Apple Vision Pro is in a league of its own !!!!!
